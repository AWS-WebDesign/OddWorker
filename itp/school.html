MORE ABOUT THE CASE

> TIME : mid 2019

> Houston School district ordered to pay 9.2 Million in a study material copyright infringement case.

> The school's principal bought a set of study material online from Dynastudy, printed them , made copies, and distributed them to the students.

> The principal did this knowing that Dynastudy states that "Copying this material is strictly prohibited".

> Knowing that he is commiting a crime, the principal even stated in an email to the school's faculty stating that "I'm ok with violating it though."

> Along with the penalty of all faculty taking copyright training each year, the school also faces a fine of 9.2 Million USD.
